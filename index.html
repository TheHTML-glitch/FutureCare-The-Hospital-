🌍 How Hospitals Relate to SDG 3:

👉 What is SDG 3?

SDG 3 stands for "Good Health and Well-Being" 🧡, part of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals 🌐. It aims to ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for people of all ages 🌸.

🚀 Hospitals directly support SDG 3 by:

  • 🩺 Providing quality healthcare for all
  • 👶 Reducing child and maternal mortality
  • 💉 Promoting vaccination and disease prevention
  • 🧠 Offering mental health services
  • 🏃 Encouraging healthy lifestyle choices
  • 🧪 Driving medical innovation and research

Without strong hospitals, achieving SDG 3 would be impossible! 🏥➡️🌎
